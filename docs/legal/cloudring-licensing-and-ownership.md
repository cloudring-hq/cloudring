## Why Elastic License 2.0

Elastic License 2.0 is the closest fit for the current #CloudRING Platform by
OPENCLOUDTECH goals:

- it permits free use, copying, redistribution, modification, and derivative
  works;
- it does not require old versions to convert automatically to Apache, MIT,
  GPL, or another open source license;
- it blocks providing the software to third parties as a hosted or managed
  service when users receive access to a substantial set of the software's
  features or functionality;
- it preserves notices and does not grant sublicensing or transfer rights.

Business Source License and Functional Source License were not selected because
their normal model converts older releases to an open source license after a
defined period. SSPL was not selected because it is a service-source copyleft
license: it can force service providers to publish their service stack, but it
does not create the same simple no-managed-service boundary.
