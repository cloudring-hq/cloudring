# CloudRING Branding

The canonical public product name is CloudRING. Use "CloudRING by
OPENCLOUDTECH" where attribution, legal notices, or public product identity
need owner context.

Short forms such as CloudRING, CLOUDRING, and related technical identifiers may
be used in documentation, manifests, UI labels, repository paths, and
compatibility references. Avoid repeating legal notices in ordinary technical
copy when a nearby `LICENSE`, `NOTICE`, or legal-document link already covers
the context.
